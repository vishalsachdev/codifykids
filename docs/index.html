    Key People

    Shreya Sachdev

    Codify Guru

    She is a fifteen-year-old sophomore.  She participated in the AI4ALL summer camp setup by Stanford at Arizona State University, where students learned python and the basics of Artificial Intelligence.  She is also an ambassador for AI4ALL. She participates each year in the First Tech Competition and her robotics team, CTRL-Y, got into the semi-finals in 2019 and to State in 2020 . Shreya is one of the main coders in the team. Throughout the season CTRL-Y helped out the younger teams of the First Lego League competition in coding, with their robots, and running the event. She has used JavaScript, basic HTML, and CSS, Scratch, and is learning Python. She is a budding poet and is also https://safesitter.org/ certified. She has experience working with kids at Camp Invention. She has helped organize fundraising earlier for the soup kitchen by collecting 100lb of food and 12 backpacks and volunteers regularly.

    Aryan Sachdev
